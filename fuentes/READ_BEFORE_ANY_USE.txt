Quenton Serif is a sophisticated typeface with a balanced personality, making it applicable for a wide range of uses. 

The design has a distinct character with decorative details. Beautiful flowing curves, the elegant curls and sharp, crisp serifs. This typeface blends formal elegance with a modern sensibility. It can be used in a magazine layout or in a novel, or to evoke an upscale feel in branding.

This font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Mans Greback has carefully crafted this serif font with attention to detail, and with a desire to make it both versatile and unique.
